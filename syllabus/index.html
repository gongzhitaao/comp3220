1 Textbook

You may need a textbook, Concepts of Programming Languages (10th Edition) (get from ), by Robert W. Sebesta. My slides are adapted from the textbook and slides from Dr. Carlisle.

2 Homework

Homework is submitted on Canvas. Late homework is penalized 40 points no matter how late it is. The last day to turn in late homework is the final exam. The homework is accessible on the homepage.

3 Grading

I have a TA do all the grading. If you have any doubts, talk to the TA first. The tentitive weights for this semester are as follows.

  • Homework: 40%
  • Midterm: 20%
  • Final: 20%
  • Term Project: 20%
